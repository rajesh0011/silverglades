ABOUT SILVERGLADES GROUP

Silverglades upcoming high-rise apartments on Extension Road is a great project that promises everything a resident wants. Set across a sprawling 10.5-acre site, this strategically planned development is designed to offer residents a lifestyle that is both elegant and comforting. With five magnificent towers and a total of 410 apartments, each unit is designed to provide spacious living spaces.

The architectural design of Silverglades high-rise apartments is both innovative and thoughtful, ensuring that each resident enjoys the utmost in privacy and space. The project features five towers with varying heights between 34 and 38 floors, offering spectacular views of the surrounding cityscape. With only 40 apartments per acre, the development ensures that residents experience an open and airy environment, uncommon in urban settings.

One of the towers is exclusively dedicated to 3 BHK apartments, with four apartments per core. These units range from 2750 to 2850 sq ft, providing ample space for comfortable family living. The layout of these apartments is designed to optimize natural light and ventilation, creating a bright and welcoming ambiance. The remaining four towers house the 4 BHK apartments, with just two apartments per core. These units range from 3750 to 3800 sq ft, featuring two master bedrooms – one grand master bedroom and one junior master bedroom. This thoughtful arrangement ensures that each resident enjoys maximum privacy and exclusivity, with ample space for all members of the family.

For those who seek the epitome of luxury, Silverglades Legacy 63A includes two premium towers that offer an unparalleled living experience. These towers feature 4 BHK + 1 apartments, each spanning 4800 sq ft. The premium towers are designed with a separate entry and drop-off zone, adding an extra layer of exclusivity and convenience. Residents of these towers have access to an exclusive club, which is equipped with a range of high-end facilities designed to cater to every need.

The penthouses in Silverglades development are the epitome of opulence. The 3 BHK tower penthouses range from 4100 to 4200 sq ft, while the 4 BHK tower penthouses offer an expansive 5800 to 5900 sq ft of living space. These grand residences are designed with meticulous attention to detail, featuring living, dining, and family lounges, as well as a power room. Each penthouse is equipped with a private lift, ensuring the utmost in privacy and convenience. The expansive living areas, combined with the luxurious amenities, make the penthouses a truly extraordinary place to live.
